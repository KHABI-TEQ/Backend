# LASRERA Partnership — Executive Summary for CEO

**Date:** 24 June 2026  
**Classification:** Internal — CEO decision brief  
**Full analysis:** `docs/LASRERA_PARTNERSHIP_STRUCTURE.md`

---

## The Opportunity

LASRERA governs **4,000+ registered estate agents** in Lagos and wants to partner on KHABITEQ’s **Verified Practitioner Pages** (DealSites) and **Transaction Registration** portal. They will act only as the **public-facing representative**—no operations, admin, or implementation. KHABITEQ retains full platform ownership and delivery.

This is primarily a **distribution and credibility** play: official endorsement converts LASRERA’s registry into paying subscribers and compliance-driven transaction revenue.

---

## Recommendation

**Proceed to MoU — conditional pilot.**

Sign a 90-day pilot with clear responsibility boundaries and a hybrid revenue-share model. Full rollout only after Phase 1 targets are met and legal review of endorsement language and data handling is complete.

---

## What Each Party Does

| | **LASRERA** | **KHABITEQ** |
|---|-------------|--------------|
| **Role** | Endorser & communicator | Operator & technology owner |
| **Delivers** | Official circular to agents, co-branding, launch/press, policy alignment | Platform, KYC, billing, support, registration processing, reporting |
| **Does not do** | Support, KYC review, payments, implementation | — |

---

## How KHABITEQ Makes Money (partnership-relevant)

1. **Agent subscriptions** — recurring; required after trial (~₦25k/month to ₦240k/year reference pricing).
2. **Transaction registration fees** — ₦100k (₦5M–₦50M) / ₦150k (above ₦50M); buyer-paid via Paystack.
3. **Secondary** — inspection fees, shortlet booking charges (lower strategic weight).

**Illustrative upside:** If 15% of LASRERA agents take a yearly plan (600 × ₦240k), subscription gross alone is **~₦144M/year** before costs—not including registration fees.

---

## Proposed Financial Split (Hybrid — recommended)

| Revenue line | LASRERA | KHABITEQ |
|--------------|---------|----------|
| Subscriptions (LASRERA-attributed agents, first 12 months) | **12%** of net | **88%** |
| Transaction registration fees | **25%** | **75%** |
| Inspection & other fees | — | **100%** |

Attribution via LASRERA referral code or validated licence number. Quarterly reconciliation and settlement.

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| LASRERA staff drawn into support | Contract: all enquiries routed to KHABITEQ |
| Low paid conversion from 4,000 agents | Trial period + LASRERA circular + optional launch discount |
| Reputational coupling on outages | SLA and pre-approved incident comms |

---

## Decision Required

1. **Approve** pursuit of MoU on hybrid revenue terms (or specify alternative: subscription-only, registration-only, or fixed endorsement fee).
2. **Approve** Phase 1 pilot scope and targets above.
3. **Direct** legal review of endorsement wording, fee collection entity, and NDPA/data-sharing schedule.

---

**Bottom line:** The product is built. The partnership is commercially and operationally feasible if LASRERA actively drives agent onboarding and KHABITEQ holds the operational line. Proceed with a bounded pilot, not an open-ended commitment.
